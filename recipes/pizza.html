Pizza

Description

Mamma Mia! This is one of the most popular dishes in the world, and for a good reason! No matter how you prepare it, it'll always taste good, even with strange ingredients, like pineapple!

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Bread
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. First, heat the oven on 250 Celsius.
  2. Prepare the bread and sauce.
  3. Prepare the cheese.
  4. Build up the pizza with the sauce, cheese, and pepperoni.
  5. Cook for 10 minutes and enjoy!
